Jujutsu Kaisen 0 Film Sells 8.85 Million Tickets for 12.3 Billion Yen to top Aladdin, The Wind Rises

The Jujutsu Kaisen 0 anime film has sold 8.85 million tickets for over 12.3 billion yen (about USD $107 million) in the 73 days since its opening. Jujutsu Kaisen 0 is now the 23rd highest-earning film in Japanese box office history, surpassing Disney's Aladdin and Hayao Miyazaki's The Wind Rises.

My Watchlist

JoJo's Bizarre Adventure

JoJo's Bizarre Adventure is a crazy popular adventure anime and my friends always recommend me to watch this Shōnen series. This anime has been on my watchlist for so long due to its vast popularity and internet culture - "Is That a JoJo Reference?".

Code Geass

Code Geass is penguinz0's favourite anime. I have heard this anime is a masterpiece, although some fans say the ending isn't great. The main character has split the fanbase into two with a love-and-hate relationship, which led my curiosity to watch Code Geass.

Bleach

Bleach is one of the "Big Three" Shōnen anime along with One Piece and Naruto, which I've already seen before. I cannot wait to start Bleach to accomplish the trinity of Shōnen anime and discuss it with my friends as one of their favourite anime.

Black Lagoon

Black Lagoon is a dark and mature anime similar to Cowboy Bebop. I am intrigued to watch it when it is available on Netflix. Black Lagoon is a relatively short series that features a strong female character, which is exceptionally rare in my anime watchlist.
